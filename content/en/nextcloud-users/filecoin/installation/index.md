## 1. Create Your CIDGravity Account

### 1.1 Open the Registration Page

Use a web browser to access: [https://nextcloud.twinquasar.io](https://nextcloud.twinquasar.io)  
<a href="img/install_01.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_01.png" alt="Step 1" width="640"></a>

---

### 1.2 Click the "Register" Link  
<a href="img/install_02.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_02.png" alt="Step 2" width="640"></a>

---

### 1.3 Enter Your Email Address

Then request a verification code.  
<a href="img/install_03.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_03.png" alt="Step 3" width="640"></a>

---

### 1.4 Enter the Verification Code

Check your inbox, enter the code, and click **Verify**.  
<a href="img/install_04.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_04.png" alt="Step 4" width="640"></a>

---

### 1.5 Complete the Registration Form

Confirm your username, full name, and password, then click **Create Account**.  
<a href="img/install_05.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_05.png" alt="Step 5" width="640"></a>

---

### 1.6 You Are Now Logged In

You may log out and close this page.  
<a href="img/install_06.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_06.png" alt="Step 6" width="640"></a>

---

## 2. Configure Your Dedicated Nextcloud Instance as a Relay

### 2.1 Create an Admin Account

Set a username and password, then click **Install**.  
<a href="img/install_07.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_07.png" alt="Step 7" width="640"></a>

---

### 2.2 Skip Recommended Apps

They are not required for CIDGravity.  
<a href="img/install_08.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_08.png" alt="Step 8" width="640"></a>

---

### 2.3 Open the "Apps" Menu (Top Right)  
<a href="img/install_09.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_09.png" alt="Step 9" width="640"></a>

---

### 2.4 Open the "Disabled Apps" Menu (Left Sidebar)  
<a href="img/install_10.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_10.png" alt="Step 10" width="640"></a>

---

### 2.5 Enable "External Storage Support"

Click the **Enable** button next to the app.  
<a href="img/install_11.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_11.png" alt="Step 11" width="640"></a>

---

### 2.6 Open the App Search Menu (Left Sidebar)  
<a href="img/install_12.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_12.png" alt="Step 12" width="640"></a>

---

### 2.7 Search for the "CIDGravity" App

Use the top-right search bar.  
<a href="img/install_13.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_13.png" alt="Step 13" width="640"></a>

---

### 2.8 Install the CIDGravity App

Click **Download and Enable** next to "CIDGravity – IPFS/Filecoin External Storage".  
<a href="img/install_14.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_14.png" alt="Step 14" width="640"></a>

---

### 2.9 Open Administration Settings (Top Right Menu)  
<a href="img/install_15.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_15.png" alt="Step 15" width="640"></a>

---

### 2.10 Go to "External Storage" in the Left Sidebar  
<a href="img/install_16.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_16.png" alt="Step 16" width="640"></a>

---

### 2.11 Fill in the Folder and Credentials

Enter the folder name and credentials from [https://nextcloud.twinquasar.io](https://nextcloud.twinquasar.io).  
Optionally check **"All users"** to share access, then click the validation checkmark.  
<a href="img/install_17.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_17.png" alt="Step 17" width="640"></a>

---

### 2.12 Confirm with Your Account Password

Nextcloud asks for it to apply changes.  
<a href="img/install_18.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_18.png" alt="Step 18" width="640"></a>

---

### 2.13 Look for the Green Arrow

It confirms authentication and access was successful.  
<a href="img/install_19.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_19.png" alt="Step 19" width="640"></a>

---

## 3. Upload a File and Access CIDgravity Metadata

### 3.1 Open the "Files" Menu (Top Left)  
<a href="img/install_20.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_20.png" alt="Step 20" width="640"></a>

---

### 3.2 Enter the CIDgravity Folder

Click the folder name you created in external storage.  
<a href="img/install_21.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_21.png" alt="Step 21" width="640"></a>

---

### 3.3 Upload a File

Click the **New** button and select **Upload file**.  
<a href="img/install_22.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_22.png" alt="Step 22" width="640"></a>

---

### 3.4 Open File Details

After upload, click the **...** menu next to the file, then choose **Open details**.  
<a href="img/install_23.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_23.png" alt="Step 23" width="640"></a>

---

### 3.5 View CIDgravity Metadata

You’ll see fields like CID, Status, and Replication progress.  
<a href="img/install_24.png" target="_blank"><img src="img/install_24.png" alt="Step 24" width="640"></a>
